Cohesive brand identity
The Challenge

Create a unified brand identity that works consistently across 13+ print and digital formats — from large-scale standees to small ID cards — while maintaining visual impact.

The Approach

Built a modular design system using a strict color palette, two complementary typefaces, and a single hero visual language — ensuring brand coherence at every scale.

02 / HERO ARTWORK

Main Event Posters

The core key visual — announcing dates and driving registrations.

The main poster was the anchor of the entire identity — every other format inherited its color logic, typographic hierarchy, and spatial balance. Dark atmospheric backgrounds contrast with glowing purple and magenta accents to create a sense of scale and excitement.
